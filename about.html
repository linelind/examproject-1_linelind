Dining With Endometriosis

A gluten and lactose free blog

Hi! I'm Emma.

In 2020 I was diagnosed with Endometriosis, and since then I have altered my diet and lifestyle to help minimise my symptoms. I started this blog to keep track of recipes I like, and to share information with others struggling with Endometriosis.

What is Endometriosis?

Endometriosis is a condition where tissue that usually lines the inside of the uterus, grows outside it. This tissue acts like regular uterine tissue does during a woman's period, meaning it bleeds, even though the blood has nowhere to go. This can result in swelling and inflammation, which can lead to a lot of pain.

There is not really much to do about the disease, but several studies show that there can be a link between the severity of symptoms and diet. Especielly gluten has been linked to a worsening of symptoms.
